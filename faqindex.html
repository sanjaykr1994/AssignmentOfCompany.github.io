  • Before Ordering Queries

  • How will the delivery and pickup be done?

    We know you need time to pack & unpack which is why we deliver 1 day prior to your travel date and pickup 1 day after you’re back. These days are rent free.

  • How will I know the time of the delivery and pickup?

    We will notify you the time of the pickup and delivery one day prior and we if those don’t suit you then we can arrange some other time that’ll be good for you.


  • What will be the quality of the products?

    Don’t worry! We only deliver products that have successfully gone through our stringent 10-point quality check process. They are 100% hygienic and functional when delivered to you.


  • By when do I need to confirm my order?

    All bookings are on first cum first serve basis. So if your travel dates are finalised we recommend you to make the rental booking at the earliest to avoid disappointment later :(


  • Is there a minimum rental?

    Yes, there is a minimum rental period of 5 days. This is to cover our basic operational costs of cleaning, maintenance & delivery etc which is the same irrespective of the number of days you rent for.


  • Is there any security deposit?

    Yes, there is some refundable deposit attached to every product. For instance, if you rent a 50L backpack, you pay a refundable deposit of Rs 600. This amount is refunded back to your bank account within 2 days after the return of the product.


  • I have some queries, will you guys help?

    Of course, we are here to help. We are just a call or message away. Call or WhatsApp - +91 7045000355 / +91 9773610355

  • Afer Pickup

  • How will you refund my Security Deposit?

    Upon pick-up of the gear, you will receive an acknowledgement email from us, notifying you about the pick-up and the damage (if any). A link is shared on this email where you update your bank account details. You can opt for UPI / NEFT and provide us with the details. Refund is processed within 48 to 72 hours.


  • What is your damage policy?

    We know usage will cause some wear & tear to the products. So we do not consider normal wear & tear as damage. But damages like visible cuts, burns, holes, broken hooks, broken zips etc are considered as damage. Read our damage policy in detail here.
