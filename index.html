About-Us

SharePal is an online rental solution to all your travel needs. To travel we need luggage and admit it no one likes to buy those travel bags and luggage. Because one they are Expensive and second it's a headache to Store those luggage bags at home once we are back from our trip. In simple words, we need them only when we travel! And we don’t need just luggage to travel, often we need a lot of travel gear too! like Tents & Sleeping bags for Hiking & Camping, Cameras & Lenses for Photography tours and these days even a GoPro to capture those thrilling moments when we are diving in the ocean or jumping from the clouds. Buying all the luggage & travel gear comes at a huge cost. So we thought to make it easy for people to travel, make great memories and worry less about the travel costs! At SharePal you can Rent all your travel needs at a fraction of their purchase cost. Rent whatever & whenever you need and return hassle free with our free delivery & return policy. Through SharePal you always get - More Choice & More Savings! Our Vision is to make things easily accessible and affordable for people through the use of technology and promoting a sharing culture. Our Mission is to create an ecosystem of sharing / renting things thereby making accessibility equivalent to ownership
